# Analysis Questions
## Project: Retail Sales Performance Analysis
## Dataset: Sample Superstore Sales Data

---

## 1. Revenue & Profit Overview
- What is our total revenue, total profit and overall profit margin?
- How many unique customers and orders does the business have?
- What is the average revenue per customer?
- How has revenue grown year over year?
- How has revenue trended month by month?
- Are there any months where the business made a loss?

---

## 2. Regional Performance
- Which region generates the most revenue?
- Which region is most profitable?
- Which region has the best profit margin?
- Which are the top 5 states by revenue?
- Which states are loss making despite high revenue?
- Which are the bottom 5 states by profit?

---

## 3. Product Performance
- Which categories generate most revenue and profit?
- Which category has the best profit margin?
- Which sub-categories are most profitable?
- Which sub-categories are actually losing money?
- What are the top 10 most profitable products?
- Which single product generates the most profit?

---

## 4. Customer Analysis
- Which customer segment brings most revenue?
- Which segment has highest average revenue per customer?
- Who are our top 10 most valuable customers by revenue?
- Is our highest revenue customer also our most profitable?

---

## 5. Operational Insights
- Does offering discounts actually hurt our profit?
- At what discount level does the business start losing money?
- How much total profit is being destroyed by excessive discounting?
- Which shipping mode is used most by customers?
- Are all shipping modes profitable?
- What is the average shipping time per shipping mode?

---

## Key Business Questions This Analysis Answers
1. Where is the business making money?
2. Where is the business losing money?
3. What is the root cause of losses?
4. What should the business do to improve profitability?
